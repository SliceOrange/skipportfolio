Who I am

Multimedia Designer based in Veenendaal

Let me introduce myself. My name is Skip Overeem. I am 24 years old. In June 2020 I completed my Communication Multimedia Design course and thus obtained my bachelor of science at Hogeschool Utrecht.

During my studies I was involved in devising and making film and photography campaigns and marketing concepts based on the request of clients. I have a broad knowledge of the various Adobe Creative tools and can therefore convert an idea into a concrete end product. During my internship at Landal Greenparks I was involved in organizing and producing photo shoots for the holiday parks. Here I gained practical experience within the profession and I was able to learn a lot from the photographers I worked with. During my studies I was trained to manage content teams.

I taught myself tools like photoshop, indesign and illustrator. After graduating, I noticed that I get the most satisfaction from creating content myself. That is why I decided in 2021 to further develop myself in this area. I started with a Web design course through the NHA and a Film and Editing course at Reducations in Ede. Here I learn how to create video content for different purposes. For example, I learn how to make commercials, documentaries and fiction films from paper to image.
